# Privacy Policy for Stadium Eye

_Last updated: 20 December 2025_

Stadium Eye respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

## Camera Permission
The app uses the device camera to capture images related to stadium observations.
Camera access is only used when the user explicitly chooses to take a photo.

## Image Collection and Storage
Captured images are uploaded securely to Firebase Storage and processed by an AI model
to analyze positive or negative observations.

## Data Usage
Images are used only to provide the core functionality of the application.
They are not used for advertising or marketing purposes.

## Data Security
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Firebase security standards.
